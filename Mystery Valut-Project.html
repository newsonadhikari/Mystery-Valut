The Mysterious Plane 914

Name of Plane: Pan Am Flight 914

Place & Date: Disappeared on July 2, 1955, flying from New York to Miami.

Mysterious Things:

  • The plane vanished without a trace and reportedly reappeared decades later.
  • Some stories claim it landed in 1978 in Caracas, Venezuela.
  • Passengers and crew allegedly found alive but confused about the time gap.

Things That Prove It: Widely regarded as an urban legend with no official records confirming the event.
